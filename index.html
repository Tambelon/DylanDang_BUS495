My Hero: Don Vultaggio

My business hero is Don Vultaggio, the co-founder and CEO of Arizona Beverages. Under his leadership, Arizona has maintained the 99-cent price point for their iconic 23-ounce iced tea cans for over 25 years, despite rising costs and inflation.

"We're giving back. We're not taking advantage. When costs go up, we try to cut in other places."
- Don Vultaggio

This commitment to affordable pricing has:

  • Built an incredibly loyal customer base
  • Created tremendous brand goodwill
  • Been featured in numerous media outlets as an example of customer-first business practices
  • Proven that long-term customer relationships can outweigh short-term profit maximization

As a business student at Illinois Tech, I admire how Vultaggio has balanced business realities with customer commitment, showing that ethical pricing strategies can be both morally and commercially successful.
